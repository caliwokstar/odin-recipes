Homemade Spaghetti Sauce with Ground Beef

Description

There's nothing like classic spaghetti and meat sauce to bring people together around the dinner table. This homemade spaghetti sauce with ground beef recipe will satisfy all your comfort food cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Method

  1. Brown the beef
    • In a large pot or deep pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Break it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add vegetables
    • Add the chopped onion, garlic, and green bell pepper to the beef. Cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are soft.
  3. Stir in tomato products
    • Add the diced tomatoes (with juice), tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Stir well to combine.
  4. Season the sauce
    • Add oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper. Stir again.
  5. Simmer
    • Reduce the heat to low. Cover and let the sauce simmer gently for 30–45 minutes, stirring occasionally. The flavors will deepen as it cooks.
  6. Adjust seasoning (optional)
    • Taste and add more salt, pepper, or herbs if needed.
